PhotoGrid
By LordGnomeMBE

I found that when I wanted to simply put photos in a grid pattern, all I could find was paid sites, or sites with lots of extraneous rubbish, so I created PhotoGrid to provide a simple, clean, ad-free way to arrange photos in a grid.

Unfortunately, due to current limitations, you must upload all the files you want at once (put them in one folder for this to be easy) and you must upload them in the order you want them to display. Screenshot this page to save your photos as a grid. More features are planned for the future. Upload some files to get started.
